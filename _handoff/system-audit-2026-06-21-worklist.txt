  • [HIGH/performance] Audit/expediente ZIP is fully buffered in RAM, not streamed
      apps/api/app/services/audit_package.py:334-355  (slice be-storage-audit, conf high)
      WHY: stream_audit_package and stream_expediente_zip claim to stream but build the entire ZIP into an in-memory io.BytesIO and emit it with a single `yield buffer.read()`.
